Why Augmentic Accounting exists.

The site is built to help accountants and finance professionals understand where AI, automation, and workflow design create real leverage inside the profession.

Built by

Bennett Bernard, CPA

More than a decade in accounting, paired with a strong interest in practical AI and automation.

Augmentic Accounting is a website dedicated to sharing knowledge and insights about AI and automation in the accounting profession.

Written by Bennett Bernard, a CPA with over a decade of experience in the accounting profession, the site is intended to bridge hands-on accounting work with the rapidly changing world of modern tooling.

By sharing practical ideas and showcasing real applications of AI and automation, the goal is to help accounting professionals upskill, think more clearly about where the field is moving, and stay ahead of the curve.
