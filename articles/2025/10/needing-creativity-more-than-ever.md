## Its Time To Get Creative

Creativity is not something you often hear associated with accounting. In school, you learn about regs and codifications that a court or committee concocted to govern accounting and tax rules of the current time. You learn how to compose professional emails to blend into the corporate scene; to not stand out as the obvious novice to whoever receives your email. Cost Accounting is done by doing X, Y, then Z. While studying for the FAR portion of the CPA exam, you learn "SAIDE" for consolidating subsidiary companies. All this to say, the traditional accounting program and typical job duties do not scream "BE CREATIVE!"

The opposite of creative is structured. The two occupy opposite ends of the spectrum.
